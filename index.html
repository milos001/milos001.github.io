Friends 0: Last Stand

Passion Project

Friends is a unique spin on the wave defense genre, a multiplayer ability based game inspired by Risk of Rain and Deep Rock Galactic. I've collaborated with a friend on some visual elements, but otherwise all systems are made by me over the past couple years. It has been my greatest learning experience in the realm of multiplayer and game design as well as the project I'm most passionate about.

First Finished Project
