My name is Miloš,
and I make games.

I'm a game developer based out of Topola, Serbia. I'm 20 years old, my favourite ice cream is strawberry
and this is my portfolio.

I went to college for a year, studying to be a mechanical engineer and all was great for a while until I started feeling constricted and not enjoying the material I was learning, so I quit. Afterwards, I tried my hand at being a solo indie game developer, video games and computers have always been a passion of mine. It went well, I learned a lot about the challenges of attempting something on my own but my income wasn't as steady or reliable as I wanted it to be, I made some games, some of which I release, many of which I haven't. Currently, as circumstances would have it, I've moved back to the rural house we own, after my mother's early departure I have to take care of my older family here and am currently looking for a stable job with opportunity to grow and learn. My ambitions are still high and I'm learning and growing every day, looking forward to the future.

HyperFeat

Pick your items!

At the start of every run you can choose between 3 different items.

Fight multiple different enemies!

Up to 15 different enemies with multiple attack patterns and unique mechanics.

Get stronger!

After surviving each 5 rounds, you will be rewarded with another choice of items.

Struggle against elites!

The further you progress the difficult enemies will become and more of them will spawn.

Slay bosses!

Random enemies will spawn each round depending how long you've survived, the strongest of them being boss enemies.

Die, and do it all over again!

Each run is unique, when it is over you can submit it and compare high scores.

More coming soon!
