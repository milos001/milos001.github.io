About Me

Miloš Milosavljević

UNITY DEVELOPER / GAME DESIGNER

I tried my hand at college, which was fun but felt too constrticting and I didn't enjoy the material I was learning, so I dropped out. Afterwards I tried making games; video games and computers have been a part of my life for as long as I remember, and I think I found where I was meant to be. I learned a lot about the challenges of attempting something on my own and through a long learning period, after making many unreleased games, HyperFeat was created, my first released title, and the first step.

Some time later I started working at Metameet, where I gained lots of professional experience and met some amazing, patient people who taught me much of what I know. After working on multiple projects for the company and some financial hardship on it's part, I started working on multiple personal projects while working on different projects as a freelancer and currently specializing as a Unity XR Developer. That's the story this far, my ambitions are still high and I'm learning and growing every day, looking forward to the future.
